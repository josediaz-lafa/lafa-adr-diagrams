Decision: 11 modules across 5 domain layers + cross-cutting — 5 built in Q2, 6 deferred. Why: Ship payroll + LTO first, expand as revenue grows.

  • agents — for AI-powered insights: inline in Q2, full module when agent #2 arrives
  • audit — for compliance & traceability: append-only event log of all state changes
  • billing — for revenue collection: usage-based invoicing, payment reconciliation (Q4)
  • charging — for energy management: EV charging network integration, cost optimization (Q4)
  • dae — for daily fleet operations: shifts, check-ins, DiDi trip ingestion, incident tracking
  • fulfillment — for car onboarding: vehicle delivery, prep, GPS install, insurance (Q3)
  • hr — for workforce management: employee lifecycle, onboarding, documents (Q3)
  • lto — for driver car financing: lease-to-own contracts, payments, collections, credit lifecycle
  • payroll — for paying 1,000+ drivers weekly: pay calculation, bonuses, deductions, Runa export
  • risk — for loss prevention: credit scoring, fraud detection, driver risk profiles (Q3)
  • shared — for cross-module master data: drivers, vehicles, centers, auth, consent
